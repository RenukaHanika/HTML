SAMAYAPURAM TEMPLE

Samayapuram Mariamman Temple is one of the most temples in South and is very near to Trichy.
The town has developed developed around Samayapuram Mariamman Temple, which is dedicated to Goddess Mariamman.
Legend has it that the present-day temple was constructed by King Vijayaraya Chakkaravarthi during the 18th century.
Goddess Mariamman is known to cure diseases and is revered as the Goddess of fertility.

SIRANGAM TEMPLE

Considered as the first among the 108 Divya Desams, a group of Vishnu temples, it is famous for the Sri Ranganathaswamy Temple, the largest temple complex in India and the biggest functioning Hindu temple in the world.

What is famous to buy in Srirangam?
Cast iron cookware, brass dice (dhayakattai), mortar and pestle, stone lamps, wooden skewers, baskets, and figurines made from 5 metals/brass.

TANJORE TEMLE

Thanjavur temple height is 212 ft towering Shiva temple, which is home to one of the largest Shiva Lingas of the country.
A great Nandhi Shrine (sacred bull), carved out of a single rock at 16 ft long & 13 ft high and standing tall at the entrance.
